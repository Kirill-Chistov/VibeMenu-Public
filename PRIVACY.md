# Privacy

VibeMenu is designed to be **local-only**. It runs entirely on your Mac and is built
so that your data never leaves your machine.

## What VibeMenu does not do

- **No telemetry** — no analytics, no usage tracking, no crash reporting sent anywhere.
- **No network calls** — the app does not contact any server, API, or backend.
- **No backend** — there is no VibeMenu cloud service.
- **No API keys** — VibeMenu does not require or store any API keys.
- **No cookies** — VibeMenu does not use or read browser cookies.
- **No accounts** — there is nothing to sign in to.

## What VibeMenu reads

To do its job, VibeMenu reads **only the local Claude-related files on your Mac** that
it needs in order to:

- show your Claude **session titles**, and
- show your Claude **usage limits**, if you enable that feature.

## Where data is stored

- Any **usage snapshots** VibeMenu keeps are **stored locally on your Mac only**.
- VibeMenu does **not** bundle or redistribute any source cache payloads or private
  account data in the app you download.

## Summary

Everything VibeMenu shows you is derived from local files and displayed on your own
machine. Nothing is uploaded, and there is no server on the other end.
